Glossary

Bit
A foundational unit of content
It is identified with an immutable id.
An aggregate consisting of many attributes
Report (a.k.a. Chapter)
A potentially hierarchical collection of Bits
It is identified with an immutable id, and lives under a Dashboard/Module
Note
A flat collection of Bits
It is identified with an immutable id.
Section
A bit group container under Report
Carries its heading, but may not contain any Subsections
Subsection
A bit group container under Section
Carries its own heading and sentiment value
Module
A facet of a dashboard loosely representing the collection of reports within
Forms a storyboard, combined with a dashboard
Dashboard
A non-complete combination of industry and region, but can be a value with no industry
Represents a highest level container of contents
